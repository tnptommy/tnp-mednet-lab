# Stakeholder Analysis

## Purpose

This document analyses stakeholder influence, interest, expectations, and likely concerns for the TNP MedNet project.

| Stakeholder | Power | Interest | Management Approach |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dr. Tony To | High | High | Manage closely |

| Misha Milanovic | High | High | Manage closely |

## Stakeholder Expectations

| Stakeholder | Expectation |
|---|---|
| Dr. Tony To | Reduced clinical downtime and better patient service continuity |
| Tommy Huynh | A scalable and secure network architecture |
| Peter Phan | Clear incident escalation, risk ownership, and compliance readiness |
| Misha Milanovic | Runbooks, monitoring, and operational confidence before go-live |
| Clinical Staff | Fast and reliable access to clinical systems |
| IT Support Team | Clear troubleshooting steps and visibility into faults |
| Finance Team | Cost control and measurable project value |

## Change Readiness

| Group | Readiness | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Executive Team | High | Strong concern around downtime and patient impact |
| IT Operations | Medium to High | Supportive if runbooks and monitoring are ready |
| Clinical Users | Medium | Supportive if the change improves access and does not disrupt work |
| Administrative Users | Medium | Need clear communication about any downtime |
| External Vendors | Medium | Dependent on scheduling and technical scope |

## Resistance Risks

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Clinical disruption concern | Clinical users may worry about downtime during migration | Schedule changes outside peak periods |
| Operational uncertainty | IT support may be concerned about new routing and monitoring tools | Provide runbooks and training |
| Cost concern | Finance may question investment size | Present ROI in VND, USD, and AUD |
| Compliance concern | Risk team may require clearer audit evidence | Maintain traceability, testing evidence, and change records |

## Communication Strategy

| Audience | Method | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Executive team | Summary report | Weekly during delivery |
| IT operations | Technical workshop | Twice per phase |
| Clinical users | Plain-English update | Before major changes |
| Finance | Budget update | Monthly |
| Compliance and risk | Governance review | At each milestone |
